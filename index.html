WHO AM I?

I'm Tegar Gustian Juliana, an 18-year-old creative mind with a passion for both traditional art and cutting-edge technology. Currently studying while pursuing my interests in drawing and generative AI.

My journey began with pencils and paper, sketching everything from nature scenes to futuristic concepts. This foundation has evolved alongside my fascination with large language models and their creative potential.

At the intersection of art and technology, I explore how traditional techniques can blend with AI-powered tools to create unique expressions of human creativity.
